About Bryan C (another-bryan)

This is a brief bio here. I help people to better understand others and the world around them, using storytelling & data visualization as powerful tools. I help everyone who is willing to change their perception of others & their perception of things that they thought they knew. Those I help become more compassionate and make better decisions.

I have a Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering, and a Masters in Business Administration with an emphasis in Marketing. I now work as a data scientist with research interests in understanding consumer/employee behavior and in improving operational efficiencies. In my free time I like to be outside, and to write code for my side projects.
